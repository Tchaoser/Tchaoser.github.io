About Our Practice

Dr. Monica Udaykumar, OD

Founder & Lead Optometrist

Dr. Monica Udaykumar is the founder and lead optometrist of Lucentis Vision, where modern clinical care meets genuine, personalized service. She earned her Doctor of Optometry degree from the Pennsylvania College of Optometry.

She has learned from the best in Palo Alto Veteran Affairs Hospital and also completed special training in Specialty contact lenses such as: Sclerals, Hybrids and Rigid Gas Permeable lenses.

After years in fast-paced corporate clinics, Dr. Udaykumar envisioned something more meaningful: a space where patients are never rushed, care is never cookie-cutter, and clear vision is paired with warmth and honesty.

At Lucentis Vision, she offers advanced diagnostics, thoughtful treatment plans, and a fresh, curated optical experience—all designed around your lifestyle and your goals.

She is especially passionate about educating patients on their eye health, managing binocular vision concerns, and making quality care more accessible to all communities.

Outside the clinic, Monica enjoys painting in watercolor, exploring Toronto’s neighborhoods, and sharing wellness and eye care tips with her online community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my eyes checked?

Children (1-4): At least one eye exam before starting school
Children (Ages 5-18): Once a year
Seniors (Ages 65+): Once a year
Adults (19-65): Every two years

Do you bill insurance directly?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please check the list of insurances we accept HERE.

Will you dilate my eyes?

Dilation is recommended for comprehensive exams to fully evaluate eye health. Please bring sunglasses as your eyes may be light-sensitive afterward.

How long does an exam take?

A comprehensive exam typically takes 60-90 minutes. We never rush appointments and allow time to answer all your questions.

Do I need a separate contact lens exam?

Yes, contact lens fittings require additional measurements and evaluations beyond a standard eye exam.

What if I need a prescription refill?

We can provide prescription renewals for up to 2 years after your last exam, depending on your eye health history.
